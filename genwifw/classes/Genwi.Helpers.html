    Genwi.Helpers Class

    Defined in: src/Helpers.js:3

    This is the helpers class object containing all the handlebars helper. To register a specific set of helpers use the following statement.

    Genwi.Helpers.initialise(["helper1name","helper2name",..,])

    or do it individually: Genwi.Helpers.subset();

    datefields

    ()

    Defined in src/Helpers.js:51

    Return the different fields of time that can be arranged to get date in any format. Use following fields to map different parts of the date. date - The present date in number. e.g 29 day - Day in words e.g monday month_num - The month in number. e.g 11 month - Month in words e.g January year - Year in number. e.g 2011 hour - Hours in 12 hr format. min - Minutes. sec - Seconds. clock - AM/PM. sup, - st/nd/th/rd to represent the date. e.g 1st.
